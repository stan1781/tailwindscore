# Tone Governance

目标方向：

- calm editorial commerce

明确禁止：

- aggressive DTC hype
- startup SaaS language
- AI assistant tone
- gamified commerce messaging
- conversion spam

## Editorial Restraint

Tone 应该：

- calm
- assured
- legible across locales
- commercially useful without pressure
- premium without self-importance

Tone 不应该：

- pushy
- slang-heavy
- novelty-first
- app-like
- overexcited
