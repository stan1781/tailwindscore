# Product Card UX

- Product cards should read like a premium collection tile, not a heavy marketplace card.
- Secondary image hover is optional and restrained. Focus receives the same media treatment where applicable.
- Hover actions may reveal on fine-pointer devices, but the card remains usable without JavaScript.
- Heavy shadows, abrupt transforms, and stacked noisy controls are out of bounds for this system.
- Swatches are supporting signals for preview, not the dominant interaction on the card.
