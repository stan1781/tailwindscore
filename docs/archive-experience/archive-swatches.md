# Archive Swatches

- Archive swatches are display-only previews.
- They may update local card media preview and selected indication inside the current card.
- They do not own variation forms, WooCommerce variation matching, or archive-side price and stock mutation.
- Their visual language should stay aligned with PDP swatches where image and color semantics overlap.
- Unsupported behaviors include mini-PDP selection flows, variation events, and bidirectional sync with PDP variation state.
