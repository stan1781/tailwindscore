# Tone Leakage Prevention

Tone leakage 指的是系统中混入不属于 premium commerce tone family 的语言。

重点防止：

- startup SaaS tone
- AI assistant tone
- hype DTC language
- pushy conversion copy
- dashboard utility wording侵入 customer relationship surfaces

## Prevention Rules

- 新 surface 先定义 tone category
- 新 helper text 先判断是否属于 governed surface
- search 文案保持 editorial commerce discovery
- account 文案保持 long-term trust tone
- checkout 文案保持 restrained reassurance
