# Trust-critical Surfaces

Trust-critical surfaces are the places where the customer evaluates safety, clarity, and purchase confidence.

They include:

- checkout
- cart
- account recovery
- payment
- validation feedback
- login/register
- order status

## Enforcement Rule

All trust-critical surfaces must be:

- registry-driven
- mood-governed
- SSR-safe
- localization-safe

They must not contain:

- inline reassurance copy
- duplicated support language
- arbitrary trust messaging

## Enforcement Priority

When trust-critical copy leaks out of governance, it is treated before lower-risk discovery or IA normalization work.
