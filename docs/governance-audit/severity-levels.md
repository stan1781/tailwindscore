# Severity Levels

## Critical

Use when:

- reassurance copy is hardcoded in template
- customer trust language bypasses registry
- preset or mood semantics are replaced by inline messaging

Typical action:

- move into content surface registry in the next enforcement pass

## Warning

Use when:

- fallback wording is duplicated
- helper tone is consistent enough but still local
- support/discovery messaging is partly governed and partly inline

Typical action:

- consolidate into existing surface family

## Notice

Use when:

- label is structural rather than tonal
- tone drift is minor
- wording is acceptable but not yet normalized

Typical action:

- defer until the surrounding surface family is next touched
