# Mobile Checkout

- Mobile checkout is one continuous purchase flow with no competing side panels.
- Sticky behavior belongs to the final action zone, not to oversized floating summary cards.
- Spacing compresses one step at a time: header, grouped fields, summary, then payment action.
- Keyboard flow must keep focused fields visible and avoid trapping the primary action under the viewport edge.
- Summary and payment surfaces should feel anchored, legible, and calm within thumb reach.
