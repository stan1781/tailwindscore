# Field Rhythm

- Billing and shipping live in distinct grouped surfaces with equal visual weight.
- Labels stay quiet and direct; helper text appears only when it reduces ambiguity.
- Mobile keeps one-column flow with compressed but breathable spacing.
- Required state is expressed through tone and concise field messaging, never loud chrome.
- Checkout-specific field styling must inherit `forms.css` tokens and WooCommerce-compatible selectors.
