# Order Rhythm

- Orders should scan vertically as individual cards with status, date, item count, and total upfront.
- Treat expanded detail as optional secondary context, not the primary reading mode.
- Keep item, delivery, and payment information grouped in calm sections with subtle borders.
- Reserve primary actions for order-specific next steps such as view, pay, or reorder.
- On mobile, stack actions full width and keep toggle language short.
